Privacy Policy for Sekant Web Security

Effective Date: 16 April 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Sekant Web Security handles your information when you use our browser extension.

What Data We Collect
Sekant Web Security does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data to our servers or any third parties.

We do not:

  • Collect personal or identifiable information
  • Store data on remote servers
  • Share any information with advertisers or analytics services

How Sekant Web Security Works
Sekant Web Security operates entirely within your browser. All analysis and detection processes are performed locally on your device. Your data and browsing activity never leave your computer.

Local Storage
The extension may store basic configuration settings (such as user preferences) locally in your browser. This data is used solely to customize your experience and is never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services
Sekant Web Security does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.
